Machine Learning Research Affiliate at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory:

In the summer of 2023, I had the privilege of joining Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory as a Machine Learning Research Affiliate. During this period, I tackled an ambitious project aimed at reconstructing CT images using a hierarchical Variational Autoencoder (VAE). My goal was to minimize radioactive exposure for patients without compromising the quality of the images. This project not only challenged my technical skills but also allowed me to contribute meaningfully to healthcare. I delved deep into state-of-the-art generative AI, trained models on computing clusters, and preprocessed 30 GBs of CT images into sinograms, enhancing my proficiency in machine learning and data processing.

Data Analyst on Molecular Dynamics Simulation at Northwestern University:

My journey into the microscopic world of molecular dynamics simulation began in January 2023 when I initiated an academic collaboration between Swarthmore College and Northwestern University. I developed a software tool that could highlight contaminant-membrane interactions at an Angstrom-scale resolution, focusing on water filtration membranes. This project was not just a testament to my coding skills but also my curiosity and drive to understand complex systems. By quantitatively measuring the hopping mechanism in membrane filtration processes, I pushed the boundaries of what I could achieve with Python and NumPy, further solidifying my interest in research and development.

President’s Sustainability Research Fellow on Sustainable Food Systems:

As a President’s Sustainability Research Fellow at Swarthmore College, I led a project that aimed to transform how our campus approached food sustainability. I developed a digital data-tracking system using Python and Excel, which supported strategic purchase recommendations for the dining department. My efforts culminated in a comprehensive 20-page report that not only raised the college’s STARS rating to Gold but also paved the way for more informed, sustainable food sourcing practices. This project was a testament to my passion for sustainability and my belief in the power of technology to enact real-world change.
